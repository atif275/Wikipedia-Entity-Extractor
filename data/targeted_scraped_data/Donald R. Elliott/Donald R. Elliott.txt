Donald R. Elliott
is a visual effects supervisor. He won at the
85th Academy Awards
in the category of
Best Visual Effects
for his work on the film
Life of Pi
. He shared his win with
Erik-Jan de Boer
,
Guillaume Rocheron
, and
Bill Westenhofer
.
